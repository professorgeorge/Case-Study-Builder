Before submitting your case study for evaluation, consider using an AI assistant like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to refine and expand your draft into a more comprehensive version. This step can help polish structure, add depth to analysis, and ensure clarity without changing core facts.

Steps:

  1. Download your case study using one of the export options (Word, Markdown, or JSON).
  2. Open the AI assistant of your choice.
  3. Copy the recommended prompt below and paste it into the assistant.
  4. Attach or paste your downloaded case study draft.
  5. Review the AI-enhanced version and make any final adjustments before submission.
